### Introduction
Ameliorate is an upcoming Minecraft Bedrock Add-on that aims to expand and improve existing features. Featuring new decorative and useful blocks, updated mob behavior, and quality of life improvements. 

### Background
Ameliorate is the first part of a big project consisting of three add-ons under the name of "Thea's Life". Building on this foundation, Ameliorate acts as a stepping stone for me to develop the principles and skills of game development for future projects within and beyond Minecraft. It started out as one aspect of one of the Add-ons but eventually culminated in its own project prioritizing updating the base game first, the point of reference being my survival world.
